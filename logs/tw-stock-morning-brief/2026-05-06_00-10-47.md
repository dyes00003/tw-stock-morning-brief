## New price-model spec
- version: `20d_continuation_v1`
- basis:
  - `monthContinuationScore`
  - `shortImpulseScore`
  - `calendarScore`
  - `secondLegEvidenceScore`
  - `relativeStrength5d`
  - `relativeStrength10d`
  - `relativeStrength20d`

### Formula
- `pullbackPct`
  - base `5.5%`
  - minus `0.06%` for every `monthContinuationScore` point above `70`
  - plus `0.06%` for every point where `shortImpulseScore > monthContinuationScore`
  - minus `0.025%` for every positive point of `relativeStrength20d`
  - clamp to `2.5% - 7.0%`
- `targetPct`
  - base `11%`
  - plus `0.18%` for every `monthContinuationScore` point above `70`
  - plus `0.30%` for every `calendarScore` point above `15`
  - plus `0.20%` for every `secondLegEvidenceScore` point above `12`
  - plus `0.15%` for every positive point of `relativeStrength20d`
  - clamp to `10% - 22%`
- `stopPct`
  - base `7.8%`
  - minus `0.05%` for every `monthContinuationScore` point above `70`
  - plus `0.05%` for every point where `shortImpulseScore > monthContinuationScore`
  - minus `0.10%` for every positive point of `relativeStrength10d`
  - clamp to `5.5% - 9.5%`

### Price-band construction
- `entry zone = close * (1 - pullbackPct) -> close`
- `target = close * (1 + targetPct)`
- `stop = close * (1 - stopPct)`
- rounding uses Taiwan-equity practical tick steps

## Interpretation rule
- `monthContinuationScore` 越高，進場回檔帶越窄、目標帶越大
- `shortImpulseScore` 高於 `monthContinuationScore` 越多，代表近端越擁擠，需要更深的回檔進場，不直接抬高 chase price
- `calendarScore` 與 `secondLegEvidenceScore` 越高，代表月內第二段行情更完整，目標帶可以放寬
- `10D / 20D` persistence 轉弱，會直接提高失效風險
